About me

Greetings! My name is François, and I am a passionate game developer seeking opportunities to grow in the industry. I have always been a console and PC gamer at heart. Along with creating small games in Java and C++ during my computer science studies, I have also invested time in following various tutorials and working on projects using game engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine. This has allowed me to gain a solid understanding of game design and gameplay mechanics.

In 2022, I participated in an Erasmus program with Glyndwr University where I had the opportunity to work on two significant game projects on Unreal Engine. In these projects, I held the role of the main gameplay programmer and also contributed to game and sound design. This experience was invaluable and reinforced my love for game development.
